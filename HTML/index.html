About Me

Who am I?

Hi, my name is Adam Koroly, and I'm 17 years old. I'm deeply passionate about computer science, especially full-stack development and cybersecurity. I love diving into both creating dynamic web applications and safeguarding systems against cyber threats. My enthusiasm for technology keeps me constantly learning and growing in these exciting fields.

In addition to my passion for technology, I pride myself on being hardworking and dedicated to everything I do. Whether it's staying up late to solve a tricky coding problem or diving deep into a cybersecurity challenge, I approach each task with determination and attention to detail. I believe that persistence and a strong work ethic are key to mastering any skill, and I'm committed to continually improving myself in the ever-evolving world of computer science.
